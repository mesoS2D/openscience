Monthly mean flux files.

Filename examples:
on_lev_nonrr_cm_flx_monthly_mean_2010-06.nc
on_z_rr_cm_flx_monthly_mean_2010-06.nc
{vertical coordinate}_{RR/Non-RR}_{region}_{fuxes}_{monthly mean}_{yyyy_mm}.nc

on_lev files are generated from daily mean flux files.
on_z files are generated from on_lev files and monthly mean q files with z as a variable (on_lev_*_mean_z_*.nc)
