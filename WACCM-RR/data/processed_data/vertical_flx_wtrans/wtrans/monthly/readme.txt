Monthly mean (and standard deviation) vertical transport velocity files.

Example filenames:
on_lev_nonrr_cm_wtrans_2010-06.nc
on_z_rr_cm_wtrans_2010-06.nc
{vertical co-ordinate}_{RR/Non-RR}_{region}_wtrans_{yyyy-mm}.nc

on_lev files are calculated from daily mean wtrans files.
on_z files are calcuated from on_lev files and q monthly mean z files .
